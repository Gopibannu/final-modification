What is Cryptography?

Cryptography is the science of securing communication and data from third parties, ensuring privacy, authentication, and integrity. It involves encoding information to protect it from unauthorized access and tampering, and is a foundational component of modern information security.

Cryptographic techniques like encryption, hashing, and digital signatures are used to secure transactions, protect personal data, and ensure privacy in digital communications.
